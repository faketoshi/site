About Faketoshi

Faketoshi (symbol CSW) is an Ethereum token distributed via Proof-of-Fraud. It maintains consensus using Proof-of-Stake (PoS) and operates as a Token Curated Registry (TCR). Combined with a locking period and fees, blatant lies — such as claims of being the inventor of Bitcoin, or even possibly (and pathetically) pretending to have a yacht — can pointed out, despite one's best efforts to block all dissidents on Twitter.
